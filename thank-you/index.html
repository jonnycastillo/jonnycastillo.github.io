Thank you for booking, I’m looking forward to speaking with you!

What Happens Next?

Please be ready to receive a phone call at your scheduled appointment time. I’ll call you directly on the phone number you provided.

Before Your Consultation:

  • Check Your Email - You should receive a confirmation email with your appointment details
  • Be in a Quiet Space - Find a comfortable, private location where we can talk freely
  • Have Your Questions Ready - Think about what you’d like to discuss during our time together
  • Test Your Phone - Make sure your phone is charged and has good reception

Need to Reschedule?

If something comes up and you need to change your appointment time, you can reschedule using the link in your confirmation email.

Questions Before Your Appointment?

If you have any questions before our call, feel free to email me at mentalpaztherapy@gmail.com.

I’m excited to connect with you and learn how I can support your wellness journey.
