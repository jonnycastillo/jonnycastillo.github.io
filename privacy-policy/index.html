Legal Notice

This Privacy Policy is provided for general informational purposes only and is not intended as legal advice. It is designed to explain, in clear language, how information is handled on this website.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Choose to Share

When you use this website, you may voluntarily provide limited personal information, such as:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Information submitted through contact or scheduling forms

Please do not submit sensitive or clinical mental health information through this website.

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit this website, certain information may be collected automatically, including:

  • IP address
  • Browser and device type
  • Pages visited and time spent on pages
  • Referral source (such as Instagram or Google)

This information is used to understand how visitors interact with the site and to improve website performance.

2. Cookies, Analytics, and Advertising

This website uses cookies and similar technologies for functionality, analytics, and advertising purposes.

We may use the following tools:

  • Google Analytics to understand website traffic and usage
  • Google Tag Manager to manage tracking tools
  • Meta Pixel to measure advertising performance on Facebook and Instagram
  • Google Ads to measure and improve advertising effectiveness (if enabled)

These tools collect aggregated or anonymized data related to website activity. They do not receive therapy content, diagnoses, or protected health information.

3. How Information Is Used

Information collected through this website may be used to:

  • Respond to inquiries
  • Schedule consultations
  • Improve website usability and performance
  • Understand how visitors find the website
  • Measure and improve advertising campaigns

We do not use website data to diagnose conditions, assess mental health status, or provide clinical care.

4. Advertising & Retargeting

Advertising platforms may use cookies or similar technologies to display ads to individuals who have previously visited this website.

These ads are based on general website interactions, not on personal health information or participation in therapy services.

You can manage or opt out of advertising preferences through:

  • Google Ad Settings
  • Meta Ad Preferences

5. Third-Party Services

This website may rely on trusted third-party services for:

  • Website hosting
  • Analytics
  • Scheduling tools
  • Email communication

These providers receive only the information necessary to perform their services and are expected to handle data responsibly.
